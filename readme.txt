A Simple Slideshow Builder! :)

== Description ==

A simple slideshow builder that uses a short code to easily place onto your website! :D

*After installing make sure you change the slideshow settings for it to display properly! :)

*currently supports up to 4 images with descriptions and links! :)

*Features<br>
** 4 Alternate layout options<br>
** Customizable height and width <br>
** 6 Different animations!<br>
** Background color<br>
** Content for images!<br>
** and more! :D

== Installation ==

1. Download Simple Slideshow Builder
2. Upload Simple Slideshow Builder into your website plugins folder through ftp
3. Place [simplepic] in your posts and/or pages, or place <?php echo do_shortcode('[simplepic]'); ?> onto your template page.
